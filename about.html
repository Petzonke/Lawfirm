About

Mukwevho Rotondwa

A black Female Admitted Legal Practitioner of the High Court of South Africa. Mukwevho r attorneys was established in 2021. The Director of the firm is Mukwevho Rotondwa. She served her articles of clerkship at The south african human rights commission from june 2018- september 2019. She was admitted as a legal practitioner of the high Court of south africa in september 2019. She joined Moldenhauer attorneys in february 2020 as a partner Till october 2021
